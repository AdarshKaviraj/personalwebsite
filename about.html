About

I'm a writer and designer fascinated by the intersection of technology and human experience. My work explores how digital tools shape our thinking, creativity, and connections with each other.

With a background in both design and engineering, I bring a unique perspective to the evolving landscape of digital culture. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to create more meaningful interactions in our increasingly connected world.

What I Write About

My writing covers a range of topics, including:

Digital Minimalism: How to find clarity and focus in an age of information overload.

Design Philosophy: The principles that guide great design and their application beyond visual aesthetics.

Technology & Culture: The ways technology shapes our behavior, relationships, and society.

Creative Process: Insights into how ideas develop and the tools that support creative work.
